Reporting a problem with your MIDI keyboard

It helps enormously if you say which browser you were using, which make and model of keyboard, and whether the on-screen keys worked when the MIDI ones did not. That usually narrows it down straight away.

Suggesting a piece

We can only add music that is out of copyright, which in practice means composers who died a long time ago and traditional melodies. If you have a piece in mind that fits, send it over.

Deleting your details

If you signed up and would like your address removed, write to the address above and it will be deleted. You do not need to give a reason.
