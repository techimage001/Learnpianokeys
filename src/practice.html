About the tracker

How much should I practise a day?

Fifteen to twenty minutes a day will take a beginner further than two hours at the weekend. The reason is that most of what you are building is muscle memory and pattern recognition, and both consolidate between sessions rather than during them.

Where is my data stored?

In your own browser, using local storage on this device. It is never sent to a server, we cannot see it, and it does not follow you to another computer. Clearing your browser data clears it, and the button above clears it deliberately.

Does the streak break if I miss a day?

Yes, it resets to one on your next session. There is no reward for a long streak here beyond seeing it, and no penalty for losing one beyond starting again. It is a mirror, not a game.
