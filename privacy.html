Your data

Privacy

The short version: your playing never leaves your browser, and there is no account to create.

What happens on your device

Sound generation, note detection, scoring and notation all run locally in your browser using the Web Audio API and, if you connect a keyboard, the Web MIDI API. No audio and no MIDI data is uploaded, recorded or stored on any server.

MIDI permission

If you connect a MIDI keyboard your browser will ask permission to access MIDI devices. That permission is granted to your browser, not to us, and you can withdraw it at any time in your browser settings. Declining it does not stop you using the site.

Analytics and advertising

The site may use standard web analytics and advertising, which can set cookies and process your IP address and browsing activity in order to serve and measure adverts. You can control this through your browser settings and, where offered, the consent controls shown to you.

Fonts

Typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts, which means your browser makes a request to Google’s servers when a page loads.

Your rights

Because we do not hold an account or a profile for you, there is normally nothing for us to retrieve or erase. If you believe we hold data about you, get in touch through the contact page and we will deal with the request.
